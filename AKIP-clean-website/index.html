Onsite & Offsite Solar Power

Solar Energy Solutions

Our customised solar energy solutions include both Onsite and Offsite Solar installations. We design, engineer, and deploy solar systems that maximize energy generation and deliver significant cost savings for Commercial & Industrial businesses.

How does solar power work? The most common way of harnessing energy from the sun is through photovoltaic (PV) panels. These panels operate as conductors, taking in the sun's rays, heating up, and creating electricity. On a larger scale, solar thermal power plants harness the sun's heat to boil water and power steam turbines.

Battery Storage Solutions

Battery storage helps businesses improve energy reliability, reduce peak-demand stress, and make solar power available beyond daylight hours. AKIP designs integrated lithium-ion based Energy Storage Solutions (ESS) for commercial and industrial energy needs.

On the Grid Online mode: Grid energy is the preferred source of energy at site, battery gets charged using grid power. On the Grid Offline Mode: Power for the load is supplied by the battery. When the ESS is no longer able to provide energy, power shifts to the diesel genset with optimized efficiency.

  • Energy backup and load shifting
  • Solar-plus-storage integration
  • Peak demand management
  • Power quality and resilience
  • Lithium-ion based ESS
  • Scalable commercial deployment
